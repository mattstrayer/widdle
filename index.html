We completely reimagined the way people use their social networks. Making it easy to curate, manage, and interact with them. With the swipe of your finger you can remove what doesn't matter and Widdle will learn to show you the things that do.

Widdle brings everything you like, everyone you follow, and all of your friends to the same place so that you'll never have to switch between apps again.

Experience each of your networks the same way you would through Twitter or Facebook by liking, favoriting, sharing, retweeting, and posting - all from within Widdle.
